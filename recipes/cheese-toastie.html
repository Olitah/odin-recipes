Cheese Toastie

Description

The classic cheese toastie in all its melted, cheesy glory.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of wholemeal bread
  • 1 tablespoon of softened, salted butter
  • 25g chedder, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil

Steps

  1. Lightly toast the 2 slices of bread
  2. Spread butter covering both sides of each bread slice
  3. Evenly divide the cheeses between slices
  4. Heat frying pan to medium, coated with olive oil
  5. Place the sliced bread with cheese-side up on the pan
  6. Fry for 2 minutes, until cheese is melty
  7. Sandwich the 2 slices together carefully
  8. Press down on sandwich with spatula
  9. Cook until cheese is nicely melted, and bread is golden brown
    • Flip sandwich every minute to cook evenly
  10. Serve and enjoy!
