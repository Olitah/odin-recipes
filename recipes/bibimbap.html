ReciPix Bibimbap

Description

A favourite Korean dish - sliced beef and sauteed vegetables on a bed of warm white rice, topped with a deliciously sunny side up fried egg.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 70g ground beef tenderloin
  • 1/2 cup mung bean sprouts
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1/2 zucchini
  • 2 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • Spinach leaves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • Sesame oil
  • Canola oil
  • 1 medium chicken egg

Steps

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker til fluffy
  2. Wash and prepare vegetables
    1. Blanch bean sprouts for 5 minutes in hot water
    2. Julienne the carrot and zucchini
    3. Mince garlic
    4. Season vegetables with salt and sesame oil
  3. Heat a skillet to medium with canola oil
  4. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender
  5. Remove vegetables and turn skillet heat to high
  6. Add beef, cook til browned, and stir in soy sauce
  7. In a separate pan, fry the egg sunny side up
  8. Serve
    1. Place rice at the base of bowl
    2. Lay sauteed vegetables and beef over rice
    3. Top with egg
    4. Add a side of kimchi for extra points
