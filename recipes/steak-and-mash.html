ReciPix Steak and Mash

Description

Straight-to-the-point juicy steak, peas, and creamy mashed potato.

Ingredients

  • 1 thick-cut ribeye steak
  • 1 cup frozen garden peas
  • 1 Yukon Gold potato
  • Olive oil
  • Salted butter
  • Sea salt
  • Your favourite sauce

Steps

  1. Peel potato
  2. Boil potato for ~15 minutes til soft
  3. Pre-heat grill to medium heat
  4. Rub steak with olive oil and season to taste
  5. Grill 5 (medium rare) to 7 (medium) minutes on each side
  6. Remove and rub with butter
  7. Allow steak to rest 5-10 minutes
  8. Throw garden peas in the microwave for a few minutes
  9. Mash potato in bowl with a scoop of butter, add salt and pepper to taste
  10. Serve on a plate with a side of your favourite dipping sauce
